Domestic & Foreign Visitor Arrivals to National Park 2018

×

Instruction

• Adjust the year range bar to switch datasets between 2018 and 2022.

• Select either domestic or foreign mode using toggle buttons; only one mode or both can be active.

• If both modes are selected, circle points will display with two colors representing the data, darker shades indicating higher values vice versa. Circle points depicted in grey represent no data.

• Hover over a circle point to view the national park's name and the corresponding visitor count.

• Click on a circle point to view the national park's image, which is clickable and redirects to the national park's official website.

• Zoom functionality is available for clearer map views of circle points or data.

• Legends and keys represent the data values for the colors used.
